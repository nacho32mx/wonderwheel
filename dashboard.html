About Wonder Wheel

Wonder Wheel is a visual, interactive spinner for creating playful prompts, choices, and experiences.

Each wheel can contain one to four customizable rings. The top pointer selects one slice from each active ring, creating surprising combinations.

How It Works

Build your rings, customize the look, hold the center to charge the spin, then release. The wheel selects a combination from the outer, middle, inner, and optional core rings.

This version is designed as a flexible tool for storytelling games, family activities, decision wheels, party prompts, creative exercises, and more.
